Googling is an essential skill in life

So here are some tips to help improve your efficiency with googling.

The #1 tip I could give is to narrow down your search

What I mean by this, is instead of posting your ENTIRE questions, only put the important words

For example, if I wanted to search for the bus communication name of the Wii, I wouldn't type into google "What is the name of the bus communcation of the Nintendo Wii". I would ask "Wii bus communication name". Narrowing down my search greatly.

This is caused by how the searching algorithm for google works, basically, it takes the words you give it, removes the filler words, and spits out a result. If we can narrow down the words it needs to remove, aswell as the words it keeps, we can narrow down our search results

The #2 tip I could give is..

to use quotations ( "" ) around the word you want to find, this'll assure that the word you're looking for is in the web pages.

Many people do not realize how effectively simple this trick is, instead of searching through hundreds of webpages for a specific thing, you can simply put the terms in quotations ( " " ) and it'll work out fine!

If you need to find multiple words, you can attach quotations around each word, optionally also put a plus symbol ( + ) in there!
